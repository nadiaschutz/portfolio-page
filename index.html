About me

a window into my soul

Curious, driven, and optimistic, I am a software developer with experience building SASS/LESS, JavaScript (ES5, ES6), TypeScript, Node.js, JQuery, Bootstrap, Angular, Ionic and React applications. Familiar with Python. In addition to web development, I also have a background in design and accounting, gaining extensive knowledge on user experience and consumer trends.
In my spare time, I enjoy reading books about programming, taking online courses on new technology, building robots and mentoring kids at programming sessions.
I love being active and try to be involved in sports as much as I can. I jog regularly, attend a gym, do MMA, and recently finished swimming classes. I relax by hiking and being surrounded by nature. It shifts my brain to a diffused mode and improves problem-solving skills.
